Game description
Cuphead is a classic run and gun action game heavily focused on boss battles. Inspired by cartoons of the 1930s, the visuals and audio are painstakingly created with the same techniques of the era, i.e. traditional hand drawn cel animation, watercolor backgrounds, and original jazz recordings.
Play as Cuphead or Mugman (in single player or local co-op) as you traverse strange worlds, acquire new weapons, learn powerful super moves, and discover hidden secrets while you try to pay your debt back to the devil!
Join Cuphead and Mugman as they battle the Devil in this throwback to the 1940’s cartoon era. In this 2D side scrolling platformer, control Cuphead and his partner in crime, Mugman as they battle the devil’s minions in order to save their souls, who said gambling with the devil was easy?
Buy Cuphead from us today and embark on this epic journey that is filled with crazy enemies, imaginative landscapes, and memorable boss battles. Do you think you have the skill to save Cuphead and Mugman from their impending doom? Then grab a Cuphead key and start running and gunning!
